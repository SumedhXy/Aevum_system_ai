# Aevum – AI Startup Refinery

Aevum is an AI-powered system that evaluates hackathon projects, startup ideas, and repositories to predict their real-world success potential.

Instead of just building prototypes, Aevum helps teams understand:
- Whether their idea has market demand
- If their architecture can scale
- What improvements are required to become a real product

---

## The Problem

Today we see a huge number of hackathon and student projects being built, but most of them never evolve into real products.

Common issues include:
- Lack of idea validation
- Poor system architecture
- No clear roadmap after the prototype stage
- Limited technical feedback

This creates what we call the **Innovation Dead Zone**.

---

## Our Solution

Aevum acts as an **AI Startup Refinery** that analyzes and evolves ideas into scalable systems through a multi-phase evaluation process.

The platform analyzes:
1. Idea viability
2. Technical architecture
3. Long-term product potential

---

## Core Features

### Idea Intelligence
Analyzes startup or project ideas and evaluates:
- Market demand
- Innovation level
- Target users
- Possible risks

### Architecture Analyzer
Reviews repository information and detects:
- Tech stack
- Missing components
- Scalability issues
- Security risks

### Survival Score Engine
Combines idea analysis and technical evaluation to generate:
- Survival Score
- Risk level
- Project potential
- Improvement strategy

### Evolution Roadmap
Provides a step-by-step growth path:
Prototype → MVP → Scalable Product → Startup-ready System

---

User submits idea and repository
